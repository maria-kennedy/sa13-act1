Meet Luna

Meet Luna, the black pug with a palate for meaty delights and a penchant for cheesy indulgences. When she's not lounging like a pro on the comfiest spot in the house, Luna is busy charming her way into playtime with her favorite human, Dad. But her true bliss? That's reserved for snuggling sessions with Mom, where she transforms into a velvety cuddle bug, soaking up warmth and affection like it's her birthright. With Luna around, every day is a feast of meat, cheese, l azy moments, playful antics, and of course, endless cuddles. She's not just a pug; she's a culinary connoisseur, a master of relaxation, a playful partner-in-crime, and the cuddle champion of the world.
