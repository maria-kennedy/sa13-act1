Luna the RavenPaw

At Hogwarts, Luna the black pug is a proud member of Ravenclaw House, where her keen intuition and curiosity set her apart. While the wizards and witches cast spells and pore over ancient tomes, Luna's magical talent lies in her ability to sniff out hidden treats and cozy napping spots throughout the castle. She may not wield a wand, but Luna's loyal companionship and sharp intellect make her an invaluable member of the Ravenclaw community. With her, late-night study sessions are never lonely, as she snuggles close, offering comfort and companionship amidst the glow of enchanted textbooks. Luna's Hogwarts experience is a blend of adventure and relaxation, where she navigates the halls with the wisdom of a sage and the mischief of a mischievous pup, leaving paw prints on the hearts of all who know her..
